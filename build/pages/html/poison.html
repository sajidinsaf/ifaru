Poison is classified as a luxurious, oriental, floral fragrance. This feminine scent possesses a blend of amber,honey, berries, and other spices.
It is the revolutionary fragrance that originated the legend of seduction.

A myth since its creation, the mysteriousness of this fragrance asserts daring and fatal seduction. This really is an invitation too tempting to resist.

This concentrated oil perfume fragrance is for women daring and provocative, enigmatic and mysterious, dazzling with its spicy heart notes.
A rich, opulent scent, ideal for cold winters and hot evenings.

Poison is recommended for romantic wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
