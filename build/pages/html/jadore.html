FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Fruity - Floral.

J'Adore is the perfume of rediscovered emotions. Designed as a bouquet of flowers, An 'Emotional... More Floral'
FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: Champaca flower, Mandarine and Ivy leaves.
HEART NOTES: Orchids, Violet and Rose.
BASE NOTES: Damascus plum, Amaranth wood and blackberry musk notes.

J'Adore, The absolute in femininity, An eternal Emotion. A fragrance legend both magnificient & mysterious. An opulent fruity floral boquet.

A fragrance that celebrates the renaissance of extreme femininity and the power of spontaneous emotion with a brilliant bouquet of orchids, the velvet touch of Damascus plum, and the mellowness of amaranth wood making it a unique contemporary scent. Confidence and sensuality are highlighted through a fragrant blend of the notes.

J'Adore is a sparkling fresh floral bouquet that is deeply sensual, totally feminine and as contemporary as you are.

J'Adore is recommended for daytime wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
