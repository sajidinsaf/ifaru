FRAGRANCE NOTES: A "pungent, powerful, mossy, musty" fragrance. The odor is heavy, earthy, musty, pungent, and penetrating. it is earthy, with an aroma that improves with age.

Patchouli, This oil from India is a light, with delicate earthy notes rounding out the soft herbaceous and mildy spicy bouquet.

A scent used for prayer and meditation. Giving one a sensation of being bathed in light and glory. Patchouli exudes a rich, earthy, rounded fragrance, and promotes a harmonious quality to concentrate and focus attention.

AROMATHERAPY PROPERTIES: Antidepressant, anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, antiviral, and antifungal; reduces fluid retention.It is believed to assist in grounding and centering the mind prior to meditation.
It is believed that, for the nervous system, patchouli essential oil helps to reduce tension, insomnia and anxiety.

Blends Well With: Geranium, Lavender, Neroli, Bergamot, Black Pepper, Cedarwood, Clary Sage, Clove, Frankincense, Labdanum, Lavender, Myrrh.
