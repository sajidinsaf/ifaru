FRAGRANCE NOTES: Rich, woody, earthy/mysterious, sweet and satisfying, warm. Its aroma is very comforting and grounding.

Khus, attar is a fragrance that is best described as deep, sweet, woody, smoky, earthy, amber, balsam. This best quality oil is obtained from roots that are 18 to 24 months old. It is superior to the oil obtained from the cultivated variety.
It is a fragrance to evoke warmth and compassion.

Khus/Vetivert is called the "Oil of Tranquility" in India. It is one of the most ancient and popular of all essential oils. It has natural refrigeration properties and is used as a perfume to cool the body.

AROMATHERAPY PROPERTIES: Deeply relaxing and comforting it helps to concentrate, organise scattered thoughts, find peace of mind and accept life's challenges. As one of nature's tranquilizers it helps to overcome intellectual fatigue. It is known to reduce anxiety, stress, tension, depression and has a balancing effect, helps to stabilize energy. It is a real comfort oil.

Blends Well With: Everything in need of a smooth and lingering base note especially Citrus and the aphrodisiac oils like Rose, Jasmine, Sandalwood, Ylang Ylang, Lavender, Cinnamon, Clary Sage.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
