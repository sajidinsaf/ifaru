FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Musky-Agarwoody (Woody)
FRAGRANCE NOTES: A mixture of Dehnal Oudh, Amber Motiyaan, Sandal, Musk and Khalta ( a rare extract from Saudi Hills), Spanish Saffron and a base of Woods, Mosses and Musks.

This Oudh-Al-Matheel attar is a rich pleasing oriental Agarwood based masculine fragrance.
With its rich and exotic fragrance, 'Matheel', meaning identical in Arabic, leaves an alluring scent suited to men and women who possess class and confidence.
Oudh-Al-Matheel, is the epitome of the classic Oudh fragrance. Steeped in luxury, the opulent fragrance creates a class of its own. It possesses a deep, earthy, musky, and potent aroma.
True to its name, 'matheel' works with individual body chemistry to create a long, encapsulating, lingering fragrance.

The long lasting base is created around a sensuous wrap of Aged Indian Amber, Musk, Sandal and Dehnal Oudh.
The generous, long-lasting aroma truly conveys a sensation of timeless glamour and mystique, giving an air of elegance that will draw attention to any person who wears it, and is guaranteed to leave an impression.

The oil is very tenacious and only the tiniest of drops is needed to fill the air with its soul evoking aroma. It is a complex aroma with many nuances, deep and ethereal.

The notes flow in perfect harmony and willl enliven your senses enrich your desire and energise your aspirations, to seek for more and more memories.


As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
