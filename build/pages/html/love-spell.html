FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Sweet-Floral-Musk

FRAGRANCE NOTES: Lily of the Valley, Peach, Raspberry, velvety Jasmin, Tamarind, Ylang-Ylang, Cedarwood oil, Moss, Amber, Musk,

Love Spell fragrance is a sumptuous floral, revealing noble accents of Lily of the Valley, enhanced by Velvety Jasmine and hints of tamarind.
It is draped in Ylang-Ylang nuances, sparked by the invigorating Freshness of Bergamot and a sweet white musky base note.
The fragrance dries down to the sweet tones of warming musk and rich amber.

This fragrance has a delicious notes that lingers on the skin, an aphrodisiac so precious it is never to be revealed, but always remembered.

Love Spell is perfect for casual and formal use.
As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
