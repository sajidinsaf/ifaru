FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Fresh-Spicy-Woody

FRAGRANCE NOTES: Nagarmot from India, with accords of saffron, clove, Bergamot, Tangerine, Ivy Leaves, Sandalwood, Oak Moss, vetiver Amber, Musk and hints of dehnal oudh.

Mukhallat Rajaali fragrance is a masterly blend of very light dehnal oud, melodiously immersing into beautiful citrusy floral notes with delicate hints of spices.
Mukhallat Rajaali is a masculine mukhallat (a perfume blend) with fresh spicy top notes, strengthened with middle spicy flowery notes with hints of clove & saffron notes.
The fragrance ends on delicate woody notes of balsam, musk, sandalwood, sweet amber and oudh.

It announces the coming of hope for lovers of oriental fragrance with a difference.
Indulge your senses in this exotic fragrance. A fragrance that imparts an aura of purity.

Mukhallat Rajaali is perfect for casual and formal use.
As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
