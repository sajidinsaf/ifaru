FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Sweet-Floral

FRAGRANCE NOTES: Gardenia, Peach, Raspberry, Aldehydes, Carnation, Honey, Jasmin, Lily of the Valley, Orris, Rose, Tuberose, Ylang-Ylang, Amber, Cedarwood oil, Moss, Musk, Sandal.

Moondrops fragrance is a brilliant burst of precious floral tones based on an artful blend of smooth floral essences.
The fragrance dries down to the sweet tones of sandalwood and vetiver,warming to musky and ambery longlastingness,which intensifies the distinguished sophistication of this sensual fragrance.

It is enveloped in a soft, sensual note that washes over your emotions and lingers in your memory as a fragrance never to be forgotten.

Moondrops is perfect for casual and formal use.
As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
