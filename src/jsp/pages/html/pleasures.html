FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Powdery- Sheer- Floral.

FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: White Lilies, Fresh Violet Leaves, Green Accord.
HEART NOTES: Black Lilies, White Peony, Pink Roses, Jasmine, Karo Karounde blossoms, Baie Rose.
BASE NOTES: Sandalwood, Patchouli.

Pleasures , By Estee Lauder is classified as a refreshing, flowery fragrance.
With this feminine scent one can feel the sheer flowering, the shimmering translucency notes of White Lily, Black Lilac, White Peony and Violet Leaves. Accompanied by a hint of sandalwood.

Pleasures is recommended for daytime wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
