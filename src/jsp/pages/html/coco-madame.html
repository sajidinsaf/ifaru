FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Fresh - Oriental.
FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: Sicilian Orange, Bergamont From Calabria.
HEART NOTES: May Rose absolute, Jasmine Absolute, Iris From Florence.
BASE NOTES: Indonesian Patchouli, Haitian Vetiver, Bourbon Vanilla, White Musk Note.

Coco Mademoiselle , By Chanel is a daring Oriental but with a surprising frashness that is both Pure and luminious.
As light as apetal, yet shimmering with sensuality. it's fresh, airy and utterly confident. A serendipitous fusion of notes, creating a unique Fresh - Oriental fragrance.

Voluptous, Seductive - but surprisingly light and fresh, at the same time. Elegance expressed in simplicity & understated luxury

Coco Mademoiselle is recommended for daytime wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
