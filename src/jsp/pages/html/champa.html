FRAGRANCE NOTES: Deep, Rich Floral Golden Champa flowers (Michelia champaca) .

The intoxicating Champa is also known as the "Flower Of paradise".

Champa attar begins with sweetly innocent top notes, then evolves into a rich, magnolia-like floral with hints of spice. The base notes help to ground the sweetness, giving it a refined balance.
The golden champa flower's sweet and spicy floral essence is distilled into soft wood notes. This delicate flower possesses a rich, ethereal fragrance that is much prized by the people of India.
This is one of the most popular and classic fragrances. A rich, sweet, and beautiful floral bouquet.

Its soothing aroma of dark woods, rich resins and warm floral notes set the perfect environment for relaxation.
It mellows into a deliciously soft, sweet 'skin scent'. A beautiful feminine perfume from India.

Wear Champa Attar made from Golden Champa flowers, closely related to Magnolias. as a lovely perfume, and also for meditation.
