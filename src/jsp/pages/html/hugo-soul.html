FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Fresh & Spicy.

FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: Star Anise, Bergamot, Cardamom, Black Pepper & Mandarin. Aromatic & Spicy.
HEART NOTES: Mace, Lavender, Coriander & Cinnamon. Sweet & Woody.
BASE NOTES: Sandalwood, Patchouli, Cedarwood, Vetiver, Vanilla, Tonka Bean, Amber,Musk & Tamboti Wood.

Hugo Soul , A game of contrasts between the lightness and darkness of its precious raw materials.
The central inspiration of the fragrance was the Tamboti Wood. This South African wood is multi-faceted: resistant, still soft, deep and vibrant.

Full of contrasts and mysterious like the BOSS Soul man.

This new men's fragrance from Hugo Boss expresses a surprising, yet balanced complicity between extreme warm sensations and refined freshness. It has strength, darkness but also is mysterious, vivid and sexy.

Hugo Boss - Soul is recommended for evening wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
