FRAGRANCE NOTES: Very floral, rich, sweet and warm, exquisitely exotic. Similar to Jasmine, yet more intoxicating.
AROMATHERAPY PROPERTIES: Relaxing, Intoxicating, Warming Aphrodisiac.

The intoxicating fragrance of Tuberose attar is sweet, floral and honey-like, with spicy and narcotic undertones.
Tuberose, Evocative of utter femininity, is a sultry, exotic scent. The exotic scent of this famous aphrodisiac flower creates an air of high-spirited expectation.
Tuberose oil soothes and harmonizes the mind. Use it to bring about a transformation of character, to dispel negative attitudes and instill a positive resolve.
As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
