FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Fresh - Oriental.(Feminine scent similar scent similar to COCO)
FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: Sicilian Mandarin.
HEART NOTES: May Rose Absolute, Jasmine Absolute,Ylang-Ylang From Mayote, Tunisian Orange Blossom.
BASE NOTES: Tonka Bean From Brazil And Venezuela, Indonesian Patchouli, Opopanax From Somalia.

COCO , By Chanel is a daring Oriental but with a surprising freshness that is both Pure and luminious.

Evoking a baroque world, COCO is a Floral-Oriental masterpiece, inspired by Venice, and the East.
Paradoxically, COCO is infused with flowers- not for their scent, but for the richness and style they give to the fragrance's inimitable signature.

An 'Anti-Floral', fragrance sensual, rich, and so infinitely stylish.
A serendipitous fusion of notes, creating a unique fresh Floral - Oriental fragrance.

Captivating, Enveloping, Voluptous, Seductive. The richest incarnation of COCO , opulent and exotic, with the richness of flowers and sensuality of Oriental spices.
COCO is is for a whimsical yet fiercely independent woman. A woman who knows the power of love and the lure of freedom.

COCO is recommended for daytime wear.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
