Incense Burners are a beautifully ornate way of filling your rooms full of wonderful fragrances like frankincense, Bakhoor. It is a great home fragrance and is brought out on special occasions like wedding, intimate moments or for welcoming guests.


It is normally to be burned with a small pieces of hot coal and some bakhoor(incense).

It is traditional to burn incense in the house and outside as it not only adds a beautiful fragrance to the dwelling, but also repels negative energy.

Method of burning Bakhoor: Take a piece of Arab Coal and heat a corner of it with a small flame (lighter/cooker), for about 60 seconds until it becomes red hot.
Be very careful not to burn onself (handle with metal tongs or similar instrument). Do not let hot coal touch skin.
Place hot piece of coal in incense burner or suitable metal dish.
Wait for 2 minutes, then sprinkle small amount of Bakhoor Incense onto hot coal. Sit back as beautiful fragrance fills the room.

We have variety of Bakhoor (Incense) that can be used in these burners. These bakhoor weave together an aromatic bouquet of raw materials such as sandalwood, frankincense and mixture of the essence of Oud, Musk, Amber, Rose etc..........it is truly heavenly.

NB : Please be careful around children as coal gets extremely hot.
