FRAGRANCE FAMILY: A warm, Spicy-Sweet-musky fragrance.
FRAGRANCE NOTES: The Krishna Musk perfume is a secret blend of Musk, peppermint, nutmeg, Warm Amber and bakuchi seed oil (India) and other essential oils.

Krishna Musk attar is a concotion of the most precious sweet spicy oils. iFaru chooses an entirely different direction and presents you with the musk that is spicy and resinous blended to perfection with nutmeg and hints of rich warm amber.
It is an highly intoxicating, rich and fulfilling attar producing an absolutely intoxicating aroma, the smell of which is sweet, spicy, and warm.

The fragrance of Krishna Musk is highly addictive and The warmly aromatic nutmeg and the warm hints of Tobbacco bring an exotic quality to the composition; the rich balsamic woods and the amber embellish the silky musk note like exquisite eastern ornaments.
As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
