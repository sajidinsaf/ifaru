FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Heavy Oriental
FRAGRANCE TOP NOTES: Rosy - Woody
HEART NOTES: Woody - Musky
BASE NOTES: Floral - Musky - Spicy

Saif Al Hind meaning Sword of India , carries an elite feel that makes it stand a class apart among all fragrances.

Saif Al Hind, the fragrance note start with rosy-woody top with a warm woody-musky heart and a floral-musky-spicy base.

The spicy and woody notes of Saif Al Hind provide strength and individuality. Its revitalizing essence creates a charismatic ambience that defines the wearer's confidence.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
