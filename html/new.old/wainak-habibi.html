FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Musky-Agarwoody (Woody)
FRAGRANCE NOTES: A regal blend of rich Dehnal Oudh, Amber Royal, Mysore Sandalwood, labdanum and Khalta ( a rare extract from Saudi Hills), Spanish Saffron and a base of Woods, Mosses and Musks.

Wainak Habibi, is the epitome of the classic Oudh fragrance. Steeped in luxury, the opulent fragrance creates a class of its own. It possesses a deep, earthy, musky, and potent aroma.
It works with individual body chemistry to create a long, encapsulating, lingering fragrance.

Wainak Habibi attar is a rich pleasing oriental Agarwood based fragrance.
An entrancing Arabian oil perfume, which travels to the deepest corner of your senses and surrounds you with a subtle whiff of Arabian aroma.

It leaves an alluring scent. Darkly sensual on a man and dangerously sophisticated on a woman, It is one of the most fascinating iFaru creations.

The generous, long-lasting aroma truly conveys a sensation of timeless glamour and mystique, giving an air of elegance that will draw attention to any person who wears it, and is guaranteed to leave an impression.

The oil is very tenacious and only the tiniest of drops is needed to fill the air with its soul evoking aroma. It is a complex aroma with many nuances, deep and ethereal.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
