FRAGRANCE NOTES: A Green, Floral Fresh Jasmine fragrance.

The intoxicating Kachi Kali is a blend of Green Aldehydes And Floral Jasmine.

Kachi Kali attar begins with a blend of Green Aldehydes Floral Jasmine wrapped sweetly with innocent Floral top notes of Jasmine and then evolving into a rich base notes of Sweet Musk with hints of deep Woody fragrance.

The base notes help to ground the sweetness, giving it a refined balance.
The gorgeous, sweet and floral essence is distilled into soft wood notes. This attar possesses a rich, ethereal fragrance that is much prized by the people of India.

This is one of the most popular and classic fragrances. A rich, sweet, and beautiful floral bouquet.

It blends well with Floral, Citrus, Green & Musky notes to yield pleasant modified fragrance blends.
A beautiful perfume oil from India.


As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
