FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Sweet, Musky, Spicy And Earthy Aroma.
FRAGRANCE NOTES: Essence of clean Baked Earth, Indian White Sandalwood essential oil.

Mitti Attar is a celebration of Life!

The rich, earthy, soothing, and exotic Mitti Attar , is that incredible fragrance of the first drops of rain that falls upon the parched earth, giving off an intoxicating aroma.
It is the smell of Mother Earth breathing and coming back to life!
Mitti Attar, is made from baked earth from the banks of the mystical Ganges river. It captures the smell of raindrops striking parched earth after a long drought.

Mitti Attar can restore our connection to the Earth, our source of life.

A nurturing fragrance, soft and mellow, deeply quiet, rich, mysterious and soothing.

This rare and exquisite Mitti Attar is revered for its grounding, balancing and rejuvenation qualities.

It is beautiful worn alone, and blends well with all the other attars.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
