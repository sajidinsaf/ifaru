Oudh Etisalbi - (Incense)

Oudh Etisalbi has natural cambodian agarwood (woodchips) soaked in fragrant oils. These chips are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions.

Oudh Etisalbi is a beatiful blend of light and sweet oudh pieces soaked in attar, dehnal oudh, sandalwood, amber, khus and many exotic flowers.
It is a great home fragrance and is brought out on special occasions like wedding, intimate moments or for welcoming guests. Women also use it to give their clothes an exotic lingering fragrance.

One of the best, rich aromatic and pleasant smelling incense (oudh chips) to be found. It smells divine and is highly recommended.
It is normally to be burned on a small piece of hot coal.

It is traditional to burn incense in the house and outside as it not only adds a beautiful fragrance to the dwelling, but also repels negative energy.

Method of burning Bakhoor: Take a piece of Arab Coal and heat a corner of it with a small flame (lighter/cooker), for about 60 seconds until it becomes red hot.
Be very careful not to burn onself (handle with metal tongs or similar instrument). Do not let hot coal touch skin.
Place hot piece of coal in incense burner or suitable metal dish.
Wait for 2 minutes, then take a small amount of Etisalbi oudhchips onto the hot coal. Sit back as beautiful fragrance fills the room.

The Oudh Etisalbi weaves together an aromatic bouquet of raw materials such as cabodian agarwood, Amber and mixture of the essence of Oud, Musk, Rose etc..........it is truly heavenly.

NB : Please be careful around children as coal gets extremely hot.
