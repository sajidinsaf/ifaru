In the Urdu language the word Jannet means heaven, while Firdaus means garden. Jannat-Al-Firdous means the fragrance of the garden of heaven.
Jannat-Al-Firdous , is a fresh and mildly sweet, soft fragrance.
This is a very popular and traditional indian fragrance. Made from various aromatic compounds like fragrant grasses, masala, tree bark, herbs, rose, and Jasmine to create a rich floral blend.

A relaxing fragrance, giving the wearer, the appearance of affluence and breeding, making one feel self assured.
