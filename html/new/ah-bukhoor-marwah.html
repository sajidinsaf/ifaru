Bukhoor Marwah by Al Haramain

NAME: Bukhoor Marwah.
QUANTITY: 18 Tablets.

MANUFACTURER: Al-Haramain (Made in UAE).

Bukhoor is the name given to woodchips soaked in fragrant oils. These are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions. A creation with care from Al Haramain.
