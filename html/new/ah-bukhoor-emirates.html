Bukhoor Al Emirates by Al Haramain

NAME: Bukhoor Al Emirates.
QUANTITY: 75gms.

MANUFACTURER: Al-Haramain (Made in UAE).

Bukhoor is the name given to fine grained woodchips soaked in fragrant oils. These are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions. These heart shaped tablets are a creation with care from Al Haramain.
