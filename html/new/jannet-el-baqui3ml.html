MANUFACTURER: Al-Nabeel / AlNabeel , from Dubai , UAE.

FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Sweet-Musky-Floral Powdery
FRAGRANCE NOTES: A precious, sheer powdery effervescence of florals intertwined with a smooth floral heart of violet and Jasmine wrapped in soft seductive cashmere white musk.

Jannet-El-Baqui attar is a sheer and sweetly sophisticated floral bouquet.
It is an enveloping fragrance that gives a first sensation of purity, like that of early morning dew and then a rich mysterious and sensual heart, with the feminine tones of the delicate musk.

The fragrance of Jannet-El-Baqui attar is very soft settling down to a beautiful sheer floral fragrance.

Delicate and sensual, like you; with the hint of wonderful memories.
It will appeal to women who are free-spirited and at ease with nature, who will love it for its sweet, dewy moments.

It is one of the most beautiful and graceful perfume. Richly captivating.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
