Bukhoor Bourmi by Al Haramain

NAME: Bukhoor Bourmi
QUANTITY: 50gms.

MANUFACTURER: Al-Haramain (Made in UAE).

Add to your dream the collection of bukhoor with Bukhoor Bourmi. Agarwood is mixed with a special kind of Bourmi Oudh attar to produce this dreamy fragrance. Bukhoor is the name given to fine grained woodchips soaked in fragrant oils. These are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions. A creation with care from Al Haramain.
