Mukhallat: means a rich blend of perfume essences for the nobles.

Mukhallat-Aseel, implies something that is Pure.
This latest rich oriental dehnal oudh based mukhallat is a light oriental frgrance that is designed as a blend of the present and the past.
The perfume has an effusive fresh top, wrapped in woody notes that give it a distinct character. It gradually builds into a spicy heart with a touch of dehnal oudh. The base is subdued, and has woody notes supported by musk and amber.
This is a scent for the individual who dares to be different.
