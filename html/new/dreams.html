FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Musky-Agarwoody (Woody)
FRAGRANCE NOTES: A mixture of soft Amber, Elite Indian Agarwood in an exciting base consisting of Dehnal Oudh, Amber, Spanish Saffron, sandalwood and Musk.

This Dreams attar is a masterly blend of very light dehnal oud, melodiously immersing into beautiful soft and sweet floral notes.
With its rich and exotic fragrance, Dreams leaves an alluring scent suited to men and women who possess class and confidence.
Dreams, is the epitome of the classic Oudh fragrance. Steeped in luxury, the opulent fragrance creates a class of its own. It possesses a deep, earthy, musky, and potent aroma.

True to its name, Dreams fragrance notes merge together so gorgeously that they extinguish emphasis of one over another. Their glorious harmony must be experienced to be appreciated.

The fragrance invokes softness of velvet and is very luminous. A gorgeous fragrance that is mysterious and elegant, definitely one of the fragrances that is timeless.

The generous, long-lasting aroma truly conveys a sensation of timeless glamour and mystique, giving an air of elegance that will draw attention to any person who wears it, and is guaranteed to leave an impression.

Only the tiniest of drops is needed to fill the air with its soul evoking aroma. It is a complex aroma with many nuances, deep and ethereal.

As this fragrance is concentrated only a small dab is required.
