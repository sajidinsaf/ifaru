FRAGRANCE FAMILY: Sensual, Musky and Spicy.
MANUFACTURER: Al-Nabeel / AlNabeel , from Dubai , UAE.

Etisalbi is classified as a sensual, subtle, oriental fragrance.

'Etisalbi' means 'Contact Me'. This exotic, yet simplistic scent possesses a blend of spicy balsamic esssences, with the warmth of African violet, sunset amber, and arabian musk touched with incense.

The Fragrance is enhanced by the unique combination of Incense, Arabian musk and Vetiver adding a fluid creaminess to warm Balsam and smooth Sandalwood.

This scent possesses all the elegance...in a smaller package. A whispering oriental that embraces you with warmth and sensuality.

It is Recommended For Daytime Wear.
