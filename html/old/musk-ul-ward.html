ADDITIONAL INFO: The process of extracting Rose oil from the petals of the flower is a very intensive process. The Rose buds are plucked when they have just opened up and before the sun's rays diminish the oils, which contain the essence of their perfume. Harvesting of flowers is done by hand in the morning before sunrise. Due to the labour- intensive production process and the low content of oil in the Rose blooms, Rose oil commands a very high price.
