Bakhoor Malikha - (Incense)


Bukhoor is the name given to woodchips soaked in fragrant oils. These chips are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions.

Name: Bakhoor Malikha (incense)
Quantity: 10 Tablets in a beautiful heart shaped box. (New, Boxed & Sealed)

Bakhoor is a great home fragrance and is brought out on special occasions like wedding, intimate moments or for welcoming guests. Women also use it to give their clothes an exotic lingering fragrance.

Indian Oudh tastefully crafted in small round tablets to give you a rich and exotic floral aromatic and pleasant smelling incense (bakhoor/bukhoor)

These tablets are individually wrapped and placed in a beautiful heart shaped box ........Highly recommended.

It is normally to be burned on a small piece of hot coal.

It is traditional to burn incense in the house and outside as it not only adds a beautiful fragrance to the dwelling, but also repels negative energy.

Method of burning Bakhoor: Take a piece of Arab Coal and heat a corner of it with a small flame (lighter/cooker), for about 60 seconds until it becomes red hot.
Be very careful not to burn onself (handle with metal tongs or similar instrument). Do not let hot coal touch skin.
Place hot piece of coal in incense burner or suitable metal dish.
Wait for 2 minutes, then sprinkle small amount of Bakhoor Incense onto hot coal. Sit back as beautiful fragrance fills the room.

The bakhoor weaves together an aromatic bouquet of raw materials such as sandalwood, frankincense and mixture of the essence of Oud, Musk, Amber, Rose etc..........it is truly heavenly.

NB : Please be careful around children as coal gets extremely hot.
