The intoxicating Ylang-Ylang means “flower of flowers” or “fragrance of all fragrances.”
Ylang-Ylang, fragrance is intensely sweet, heady, floral, and slightly spicy, with a narcissus or bananalike overtone.

Ylang-Ylang is a pale yellow oil carring an intoxicating scent that is sweet, floral and as exotic as the islands of its origin.

Reminiscent of Jasmine, sometimes called "The Poor Man's Jasmine", Ylang-Ylang's aroma is sweet and exotically floral. Heavier than most florals.

This fragrance is traditionally used in aromatherapy to sharpen the senses and to temper depression, fear, anger, and jealousy. It also has a reputation as an aphrodisiac.
