Ood Maamul - (Incense)


Maamul is the name given to woodchips soaked in fragrant oils. These chips are burned in incense burners to perfume the home and clothing specifically on special occasions.

Ood Maamul Mamool is a beatiful blend of light and sweet oudh pieces soaked in attar, dehnal oudh, sandalwood, amber, khus and many exotic flowers.

Maamul is used in the Middle East to scent one's home, linen and clothing.
This Maamul is a superior blend of High quality oudh powder mixed with oudh oil, rose, sandal, amber and other ingredients to give you the best of Arabian Perfumes Maamul. Maamul is best for home, office, cloths and your skin. It is usually burned in a Mabkara, traditional incense burner.

Experience this Exotic Fragrance Today! This is one of the best, rich aromatic and pleasant smelling incense (Maamul) to be found.......Highly recommended.
It is normally to be burned on a small piece of hot coal.

It is traditional to burn incense in the house and outside as it not only adds a beautiful fragrance to the dwelling, but also repels negative energy.

Method of burning Maamul: Take a piece of Arab Coal and heat a corner of it with a small flame (lighter/cooker), for about 60 seconds until it becomes red hot.
Be very careful not to burn onself (handle with metal tongs or similar instrument). Do not let hot coal touch skin.
Place hot piece of coal in incense burner or suitable metal dish.
Wait for 2 minutes, then sprinkle small amount of Ood Maamul Incense onto hot coal. Sit back and enjoy as the beautiful fragrance fills the room.

The Maamul weaves together an aromatic bouquet of raw materials such as sandalwood, frankincense and mixture of the essence of Ood, Musk, Amber, Rose etc..........it is truly heavenly.

NB : Please be careful around children as coal gets extremely hot.
